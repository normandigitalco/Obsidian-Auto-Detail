Our Story

BUILT FOR
THE OBSESSED.

Obsidian was founded by car enthusiasts who were tired of settling. We built the studio we always wished existed right here in Frisco, TX.

The Mission

WHY WE
DO THIS

We started Obsidian because we believed DFW deserved a real detailing studio — not a spray-and-wipe operation, not an upsell machine, but a genuine craft service run by people who love cars as much as you do.

Every product we use is chosen for performance over margin. Every process we follow is designed to protect your paint first. We turned down the volume on the sales pitch and turned up the quality on the work.

We're a small, intentional operation — we limit daily appointments so every car leaves the way we'd want our own to look. That's not a marketing line. It's how we've earned a 5-star reputation across DFW.
